We enable structured career exploration and informed choice for young people in government schools across India at scale. By partnering with governments and credible NGOs, creating contextualized content, and building the capacity of teachers to facilitate our program.

What We Do

हम क्या करते हैं

Structured Career Exploration

हम छात्रों को करियर की संरचित खोज और सूचित विकल्प चुनने में मदद करते हैं

We create contextualized content and build the capacity of teachers to facilitate career exploration programs. Our approach helps students understand their strengths and make informed career choices.

Collaborative Large-Scale Programs

सरकार और प्रतिष्ठित NGOs के साथ साझेदारी करके बड़े पैमाने पर कार्यक्रम

We partner with the Government of Delhi, and run District-Wide Career Support Programs in Haryana, Uttarakhand, and Madhya Pradesh to reach students at scale.

Other Initiatives by Alohomora

अलोहोमोरा की अन्य पहल

After School Support

स्कूल के बाद सहायता

Support program for students after class 12th. Helps young people to connect with each other. Also provides space to link with different skilling institutes, vocational courses and scholarships.

Delhi Govt Programs

दिल्ली सरकार के कार्यक्रम

Delhi Govt with Alohomora launched Desh ke Mentor (DKM) and Entrepreneurship Mindset Curriculum (EMC) for students in 9th-12th classes. DKM connects students with successful youth (mentors) to support student growth.
